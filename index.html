What Is Tarot Card Reading?

Tarot card reading is an age-old practice that uses a deck of symbolic cards to explore your subconscious mind and gain insight into present circumstances and potential futures. With its roots in medieval Europe and its incorporation into spiritual mysticism, tarot has evolved into a powerful tool for self-reflection, decision-making, and empowerment.
